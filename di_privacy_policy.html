Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 3, 2026  ·  Version 1.0

Daily Islam is built on a single principle: your data belongs to you. The app collects no personal information, sends no data to any server, and contains no advertising or analytics of any kind. This policy explains exactly what happens — and what does not happen — with your information when you use Daily Islam.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any account information
  • Your device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, etc.)
  • Your location coordinates or GPS history
  • Your usage patterns, feature interactions, or session data
  • Crash logs or diagnostic data sent to our servers
  • Any information about your device, operating system, or hardware

2. Device Permissions

Location (GPS)

Daily Islam requests location permission solely to calculate the five daily prayer times for your current position, the Qibla compass bearing to Mecca, and Suhoor and Iftar times during Ramadan.

Your coordinates are computed on-device and are never transmitted to Zero Square Technologies or any third party. The last known location is cached locally on your device so prayer times remain available in offline or airplane mode. You may deny this permission and enter your city or coordinates manually — the app remains fully functional.

Notifications

Used only to deliver adhan alerts at prayer times. No notification content or delivery data is sent to any remote server.

Storage / Local Database

Used to persist your Quran bookmarks, last-read position, Tasbeeh counter state, Habit Tracker logs, Zakat history, and app preferences — all stored exclusively on your device.

3. Internet Access

Daily Islam functions fully offline. An internet connection is optionally used only for one purpose: fetching the current gold and silver prices to update the Zakat calculator's nisab threshold from a public financial API. This request contains no user-identifying information and can be skipped entirely by staying in offline mode, where the last cached nisab value is used.

No other network requests are made. There are no analytics calls, no advertising network calls, and no telemetry of any kind.

4. Third-Party SDKs and Advertising

Daily Islam contains no third-party advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, and no tracking libraries. We do not monetise user data in any form. The app is and will remain completely free of ads.

5. Data Stored on Your Device

All app data is stored locally and remains under your full control. Uninstalling the app removes all of it from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

Daily Islam does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is suitable and safe for all ages.

7. Security

Because Daily Islam does not transmit personal data, there is no server-side data to breach. All content stored on your device is protected by your device's own security mechanisms — screen lock, full-disk encryption, and Android/iOS sandboxing.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future app version, we will update the effective date at the top of this document and note the change in the app's Play Store release notes. Continued use of the app after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
